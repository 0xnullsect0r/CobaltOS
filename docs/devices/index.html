💻 Supported Devices

CobaltOS supports all Chromebooks compatible with MrChromebox's UEFI Full ROM firmware — covering most Chromebooks manufactured from 2012 onwards.

ℹ️

Support is identified by board name, not model name

Many Chromebooks share hardware under different product names. Find your board name using the steps below, then look it up in the table.

🚀

CobaltOS v1.4.0 — latest release

All listed boards receive full hardware support: keyboard remapping, audio UCM profiles, touchpad tuning, 3-finger COSMIC workspace gestures, and — on btrfs installs — automatic pre-upgrade snapshots for safe rollback. Install guide →

🔍 How to find your board name

  1. Turn on your Chromebook and make sure it has booted into ChromeOS.
  2. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://version — look for the Platform or Firmware Version field. Your board name appears there (e.g. BOBBA).
  3. Alternatively: hold Esc + Refresh ↻ + Power to enter Recovery Mode. Your board name is printed at the bottom of the screen.
  4. On tablet-form Chromebooks (no keyboard): hold Volume Up + Volume Down + Power instead.
